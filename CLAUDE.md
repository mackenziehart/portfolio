# CLAUDE.md — Mackenzie Hart Portfolio

## What this is
Portfolio site for Mackenzie Hart, Product Designer. 
Showcases UX/interaction design work for recruiters and collaborators.

## Tech stack
- Astro + vanilla CSS/JS
- Deploying to GitHub Pages initially, may move to custom domain later
- No frameworks beyond Astro

## Pages & structure
- **Landing page** — hero, listed case studies
- **Case study pages** — one per project (see below)
- **About page** — photo, brief bio, interactive resume

## Aesthetic direction
Quiet and considered. Generous negative space, light backgrounds, 
restrained typography. Moments of unexpected warmth or texture — 
not decorative but purposeful. The site should feel like the work: 
emotionally attentive, low friction, intentional.

References: Japanese minimalist print design, archival document 
aesthetics, close-cropped moody photography.

## Case studies
1. **Rume** — A contemplative memory collection app
2. **Aether** — An app that assesses social rhythm and provides 
   mindful methods to benefit mental health
3. **Greenroom** — A concert logging app for live music lovers
4. **Icon project** — Icon designed at work that shipped in a 
   real product (May 2026)

## Constraints & preferences
- Prioritize performance and clean HTML
- Accessibility matters
- Keep interactions purposeful, not decorative
